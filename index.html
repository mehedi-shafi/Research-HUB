What is DIU Research-HUB

Take a tour at DIU Research-HUB.

There's a lot of students who are qualified and expert in particular sectors looking for supervisors for getting involve in Research. And some teachers also looking for co-authors for their research projects. DIU Research-Hub is platform to colaborate both of them. It's a platform to find out your supervisor or your co-author.




  • Supervisors

    Students can find out their expected supervisors from supervisor list and also apply to do reseacrh work under them applying according to their skills.

  • Co-authors

    Supervisor can select their students from the application requested by students and also directly select from the sudents list. Research hub can act as intermediary or they can directly contact over phone or email.

Ongoing Projects

Projects that are ongoing under the supervision of supervisors

Bangla-Bochon: The First Bangla Open Access Isolated Sign Language Dataset

Supervisor: Dr. Syed Akhter Hossain
Co-authors: Sheikh Abujar ,Akm Shahariar Azad Rabby, Sanzidul Islam, Sadia Sultana Sharmin, Nazmul Ahsan.

Overview:
We are going to introduce the first open access Bangla sign language digits dataset for BdSL. This dataset was collected from different students of different deaf schools at Dhaka from variety of aged groups.

Passport verification with blockchain technology

Supervisor: Dr. Touhid Bhuiyan
Co-authors: Delwar Alam, Md Alamgir Kabir, Tanjila Farah, Moniruz Zaman.

Overview:
Developing a solution that combines passport verification with blockchain technology. The goal is to put an end to manual passport verification checks for seamless entry.

Sentiment Analysis of Tweet Data: The Study of Sentimental State of Human from Tweet Text

Supervisor: Dr. Syed Akhter Hossain
Co-authors: S. M. Mazharul Hoque Chowdhury, Priyanka Ghosh.

Overview:
Finding a new and much more accurate data analysis tool based on a huge amount of Tweet data. The study will show the different sentiment state of human in different times based on their tweets.
